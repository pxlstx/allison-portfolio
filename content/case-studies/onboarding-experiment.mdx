## Overview

New users were signing up but stalling before their first meaningful action. I designed a lightweight onboarding experiment to reduce time-to-value.

## Challenge

The existing empty state assumed too much product knowledge. Analytics showed a steep drop-off between account creation and the first completed project.

## Approach

I storyboarded three onboarding variants, ran unmoderated tests, and partnered with growth to ship a staged rollout. Copy and progressive disclosure did more work than extra UI chrome.

## Outcome

- 18% lift in week-one activation
- Median time-to-first-project down from 12 minutes to 6
- Patterns reused in two adjacent product areas
